Future with AI and AR.

With Armis tech’s AI -powered system , we aim to understand user’s behaviour while the use of a certain system. We can help users to easily navigate systems which can increase usabilty and accessibilty for everyone with multiple languages from a single interface.Designers working with AI can create designs faster and more cost effective to get an increased speed and efficiency.

The power of AI will lie in the speed in which it can analyze vast amounts of data and suggest design adjustments. A designer can then cherry-pick and approve adjustments based on that data to help users understand more about our products.

Augmented reality (AR) is an experience where designers enhance parts of users’ physical world with computer-generated input. Designers cancreate in digital content which responds in real time to changes in the user’s environment, typically movement.

Armis aims to make this possible for our users in our products to get the ultimate experince of AR in your hands.

Our Products

Roam is a personalised travel app for domestic tourist. In order for Roam to create tailor-made system , our team listens to what the customer want and then create experiences that’s right for them and their budget so far customers have been doing offline via calling or using international platforms. However, with easy acess to internet customers have become more visual to how they travel and stay.

goRoam is an extension service of Roam. Users will be able to view easily most of the clubs and bars around the valley and the parties. This is aone stop solution for party goers who want information in palm of their hands.
