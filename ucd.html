UCD intoduction

User-centered design (UCD) helps designers gain those crucial insights into who their users are and how they use the product then assists them in incorporating that knowledge into their design process. It is an iterative process as designers focus on the users and their needs .It involves research and techniques which can help create accessible products.

How does the process work for us.

This is where designers research who their users are what kind of problems to they face and have , what motivates them to use a specific product. It is very helpful to have a good understanding of UX strategy it helps bridge between users and the goals of a business. the design process starts with user reasearch, surveys , interviews and then moves onto user persona creation, information architecture ,user flow creation ,affinity mapping ,wireframing prototyping and testing. Once this is done it is to evalute the effectiveness of the usability of the product and the iterate.
